Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 22, 2026

Welcome to Kinla Services. Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and services in Kinniya, Sri Lanka.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with efficient domestic and digital services:

  • Personal Details: Full name and contact number for identity and communication.
  • Location Data: Physical address for on-site services (Plumbing, Electrical, CCTV setup, etc.).
  • Work References: Optional images or photos uploaded by you to help our technicians understand the job requirements before arrival.
  • Account Info: Email address for secure authentication via Google/Email.

2. Use of Information

The information we collect is used to:

• Match your request with the right technician.
• Send booking confirmations and status updates.
• Allow technicians to locate your premises accurately.
• Improve our app functionality and user experience.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your contact details and address are shared only with the verified Kinla technician assigned to your specific booking. We use Google Firebase for secure data storage and real-time synchronization.

4. Account Deletion & Data Removal

Your Right to be Forgotten: We respect your control over your data. You may request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time.

To request account deletion, go to Settings > Customer Support within the app and contact our team. Once a request is verified, we will remove your profile, saved addresses, and history from our active databases within 7 business days.
